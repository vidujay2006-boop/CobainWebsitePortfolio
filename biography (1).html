Biography

The making of a defining artist

Kurt Donald Cobain was born on 20 February 1967 in Washington, USA. He developed a deep connection to music early in life, shaped by family influence and the artists he admired.

Formation of Nirvana

In Aberdeen high school, Cobain met Krist Novoselic. Their shared interest in underground and rock music became the foundation for Nirvana. The early period was driven by demos, practice sessions, and experimentation.

Rise to Fame

With Dave Grohl joining on drums, the trio reached global audiences and quickly became one of the defining bands of the era.

Influence and Legacy

Cobain remains one of the most influential musicians of the 20th century. His songwriting, voice, and stage presence continue to shape new generations of artists.
